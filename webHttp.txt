GOAL:
convert soap access to REST access by adding and defining restful addresses to the service interface definition

<endpoint address ="REST" binding="webHttpBinding" contract="wcfLibrary.IService" behaviorConfiguration="WEB"></endpoint>
<endpointBehaviors>
        <behavior name="WEB">
          <webHttp/>
        </behavior>
      </endpointBehaviors>


how to find the address:
and after adding web reference and simlated related address depend on IIS/console host
on IIS:
http://localhost:88/customService.svc/REST/Interface1

NOTE:
webHttpBinding [for rest] wont be published on wsdl
